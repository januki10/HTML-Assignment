THREATS
TO TURTLES'

Threats to sea turtles have a profound impact on their overall well-being and survival.These threats include:fisheries bycatch, pollution, climate change, habitat loss and degration,illegal trade and poaching.Conservation efforts aim to mitigate these threats through habitat conservation, pollution reduction, the implementation of turtle-friendly fishing practices, and the enforcement of protective measures to ensure the long-term survival of these incredible creatures.
